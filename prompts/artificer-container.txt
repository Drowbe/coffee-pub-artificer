===============================
JSON TEMPLATE FOR CONTAINERS
===============================

Use namespaced Artificer flags: artificerType "Tool", artificerFamily "Container" or "Apparatus". Include artificerTraits for recipe matching.

FAMILY (artificerType "Tool" only):
- Container: Vials, herb bags, mixing bowls — items that hold/carry results
- Apparatus: Beakers, mortar and pestle, crucibles — items used to process (heat, grind)

ARTIFICER TRAITS for recipe matching (2–5 strings):
- Beaker: ["Liquid", "Heat-Safe", "Glass"]
- Vial: ["Liquid", "Small", "Glass"]
- Mortar and Pestle: ["Grinding", "Dry", "Ceramic"]
- Crucible: ["Metal", "High-Heat"]
- Mixing Bowl: ["General", "Mixing", "Ceramic"]
- Herb Bag: ["Herbal", "Infusion", "Cloth"]
